What is Online Fraud?

Welcome to our webpage dedicated to understanding the cutting-edge techniques employed in the battle against online fraud. In today's interconnected world, the convenience of online transactions is undeniable. However, it comes with the challenge of combating ever-evolving cyber threats. We are here to shed light on how Machine Learning, specifically Decision Tree Classifiers, is revolutionizing online security. Decision Tree Classifiers are at the forefront of fraud detection, providing a simple yet robust solution to identify fraudulent activities. By analyzing transaction data, these intelligent algorithms make decisions based on a series of questions, ensuring swift and accurate detection.The art of fraud detection lies in understanding transaction features. Decision trees excel in analyzing transaction amounts, locations, timestamps, user behavior patterns, and more. This deep understanding allows for the creation of precise fraud detection models.
